Flip. Learn. Improve. Memorize. Own.

Create your own word collections and memorize vocabulary at your own pace. Flip the cards, mark what you know, and focus on the words that need more practice.

How it works

A simple way to build, practice, and master your own word collections.

Create Collection

Create a collection for the words you want to memorize and add your word pairs in any language.

Practice with Flashcards

Your words appear randomly as two-sided flashcards. Flip each card to reveal its meaning and test yourself actively.

Sort What You Know

Swipe right if you know the word. Swipe left if you don’t. Each choice helps you learn actively.

Focus and Repeat

Words are automatically organized into sub-collections based on your answers. Focus on the ones you struggle with and practice again.
